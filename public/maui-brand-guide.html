Maui Main

"Hi!" - Your friendly neighborhood grooming guide

Maui is a small white/cream fluffy Maltese-type pup. He's the face of Groomingdale's - warm, approachable, and always happy to help pet parents find the perfect groomer. His signature red bandana says "MAUI" with a paw print.

This is the primary mascot pose used for hero sections, navigation, empty states, and all brand touchpoints. He's waving hello with a speech bubble.

Maui Usage Guidelines

DO
  • Always include "MAUI" text on the bandana in Fredoka Bold
  • Remove bandana for water-related poses (bath, blow dry, towel)
  • Keep thick maroon #38001D outlines consistent
  • Use cream body with tan shading for depth
  • Maintain warm, friendly expressions in every pose
  • Include paw print icon on bandana next to text
  • Use Maui at appropriate sizes (min 64px, hero 160px+)
DON'T
  • Don't change Maui's breed or proportions
  • Don't use thin outlines - thick maroon only
  • Don't make the nose oversized or brown (small, dark, round)
  • Don't put Maui on groomer listing/hero cards
  • Don't stretch, skew, or apply filters to the illustrations
  • Don't change bandana color from red #C1272D
  • Don't use Maui in a way that feels like a SaaS product mascot
