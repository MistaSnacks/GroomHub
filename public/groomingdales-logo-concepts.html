Design System Notes

  • Font: Fredoka Bold 700 — consistent with all brand headings and Maui's bandana
  • Color split: "Grooming" in brand maroon #38001D, "dale's" in brand amber #F5A623
  • On dark: "Grooming" flips to white, amber stays for contrast
  • Mascot integration: Concepts 2, 4, 5 include Maui directly in the logo
  • Favicon: Gold "G" on maroon — recognizable at all sizes, uses the amber accent
  • Teal accent: Concept 5 uses brand teal #4ECDC4 for the tagline as a secondary color pop
  • Directory feel: All concepts prioritize warm, approachable over corporate/SaaS
